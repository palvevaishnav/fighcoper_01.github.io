Kakashi Hatake

Leaf Shinobi

One of the most beloved characters in the series, known for his stoic demeanor, sharp wit, and exceptional ninja skills. Kakashi is a highly skilled ninja who served as the leader of Team 7, which consists of the series' main protagonists: Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno. He is also known as the "Copy Ninja" due to his ability to copy any technique he sees.With his trademark mask covering half of his face and his distinctive silver hair.
